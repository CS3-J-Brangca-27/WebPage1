Jane's Hooks is a small business by Jaira Jane A. Brangca, a Pisay scholar, who lives in Philippines, Butuan City. "I made this business for fun and to earn some extra money. ", said Jaira. Crocheting was just a hobby that I tried to keep my self accompanied during the pandemic, but with my dream to start a business and my small hobby, I was able to start Jane's Hooks.
